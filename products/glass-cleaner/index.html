About the Product

Clean Max Glass Cleaner is a powerful multipurpose cleaner designed to deliver streak-free, sparkling shine on glass and shiny surfaces. Its ammonia-free formula ensures safe and effective cleaning for homes, offices, and commercial spaces.

Composition

  • Benzalkonium Chloride
  • Non-ionic Surfactants
  • Ethyl Alcohol
  • Hydrogen Peroxide
  • DM Water
  • Fragrance & Color

How to Use

  1. Turn nozzle to spray or stream position
  2. Spray directly on surface
  3. Wipe with clean, dry cloth
  4. Buff lightly for extra shine

Safety Instructions

  • For external use only
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Do not ingest
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Do not use on electronic screens
